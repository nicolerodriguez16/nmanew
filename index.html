About the Exhibition

Sonder is the realization that each random passerby is living a life as vivid and complex as your own.

This exhibition explores how individual identity and social issues shape the way we operate. In this exhibition, each artist chose a topic that resonates deeply with their own life experiences and passions.

Sonder: The Human Experience highlights the idea that every person creates a complex story that deserves to be seen and felt. Together, these works create a shared space that allows a glimpse into the vibrant and sometimes painful aspects of life, and the meaningful realities that make up the human experience.
